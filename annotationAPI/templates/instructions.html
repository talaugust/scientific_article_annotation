Instructions

In this task, you will be asked to do the following:

  1. Read a news article about a medical or scientific finding.
  2. Highlight sentences of the news article and answer questions about the article.
  3. Answer a set of demographic questions.

How to highlight sentences

During the expirement you will be shown a news article, to the side of the news article will be instructions on which sentences to highlight in the article. Please follow these instructions for highlighting sentences.

Please highlight full sentences in the article. If there are two or more sentences next to each other that both fit the instructions for highlighting, please highlight and label them together .

A sentence can fall into multiple categories, in which case highlight it once for each category.

These instructions will also posted above the article, in case you want to check them while reading and completing the study. Good luck!
